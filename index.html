Jun 04, 2025 Optimizing Temporal Hypergraph Paths: A Time-Aware Hyperedge Hopping Approach - This paper addresses the challenging problem of optimal pathfinding in temporal hypergraphs, which model complex multi-way relationships evolving over time. We introduce the Time-Aware Hyperedge Hopping (TAHH) algorithm, a novel and efficient Dijkstra-like approach designed to find paths that minimize the final arrival time in these dynamic higher-order networks.
